ABOUUT GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC,KHARSAWAN

Government Polytechnic Kharsawan is a thirteenth Polytechnic in the state of Jharkhand. It came into existence in the year 2006. The Institution is offering Diploma Programmes in Computer Engg., Electronics & Communication Engg. and Electricals Engg. Presently the Institution building is in the 5 acres of land and there is additional 5 acres of land where complete building has to be developed.

In order to produce diploma level technical man power of desired quality and standard institution has qualified faculty and other resources to suit with fast changing technological innovation and inventions.

Programme Structure and Contents have been revised recently with the help of experienced faculty in the guidance of NITTR Kolkata.

DEPARTMENTS OF GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC , KHARSAWAN

COMPUTER ENGINEERING

1. he Dept. of Computer Engineering caters the most demanding professionals with cutting edge technology with its experienced faculty and fully equipped laboratories. The department’s mission is to advance, evolve and enhance computer science and computing engineering fundamentals to build the intellectual capital of our research and our students. The Diploma in Government Polytechnic, Kharsawan is a broad and flexible diploma program with the curriculum specifically designed to reflect the depth and breadth of computer engineering. Our endeavor is to make the CSE Department of Government Polytechnic, Kharsawan an important resource centre for the development of computing and IT systems and applications. CSE Department boasts a vibrant faculty.

2. Government Polytechnic, Kharsawan has 60 seats in ComputerEngineering Department for each semester.



ELECTICAL ENGINEERING

1. The field of Electrical Engineering being one of the core areas of engineering has great demand even in this age of information technology. The recent slowdown of IT market has revived the interest of the engineering education aspirants with a renewed vigour. Although the focus of the world shifted to other fields of engineering for some period of time in the past few decades, the importance of it has never been diminished and neither it will be. One can see the application of the principles of Electrical engineering in the field of telecommunication and instrumentation. So, good prospects of employment are always there for the graduates in these disciplines across the sectors like telecommunication, instrumentation and information technology.

2. Government Polytechnic, Kharsawan has 60 seats in Electronics and Communication Department for each semester.



ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

1. Electronics and Communication Engineering is a rapidly progressing field. With technology enveloping our everyday life, opportunities for electronic engineers are endless. The ECE Dept at Government Polytechnic, Kharsawan prepares students for careers in this constantly evolving discipline. The department offers Diploma in ECE with full and flexible diploma curriculum. The Dept of Electronics & Communication Engineering has proficient and skilled faculty to impart world class technological solutions to the students. The department is accomplished with well equipped state of the art global laboratories.

2. Government Polytechnic, Kharsawan has 60 seats in Electricals Engineering Department for each semester.



FACULITES OF GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC , KHARSAWAN

G.P. KHARSAWAN HOSTLE

The College Hostel, located within the college campus, consists of two blocks, one each for boys and girls. It is a residence hostel for students admitted to Govt. Polytechnic Kharsawan.


G.P. KHARSAWAN LIBRARY

The library offers a range of space for study. You will find the beautiful library reading room and an open study area , and our bright and modern library is a flexible study space arranged for you to work in a way that suits you.


MORDEN COMPUTER SYSTEMS

The computer centre of this institute boasts of being called upon “The gateway across the globe”. With over 200 computers, ready to take the students across the geographical boundaries of the land at a speed of 10Mbps dedicated internet leased line.


WI-FI CAMPUS

The entire campus is Wi-fi enabled with high speed internet connection to allow the students to access the internet no-matter wherever they are. The coverage is not just limited to the classrooms, instead it extends to all the facilities within the campus premises.
